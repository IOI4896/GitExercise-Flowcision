Conclusion

From the analysis above, most existing applications focus on specific aspects such as time management, task organization, or habit tracking. However, they do not integrate multiple factors such as study time, sleep, focus, and stress into a unified evaluation system.

Our project aims to address this gap by developing a decision-based simulation system that evaluates multiple factors and provides personalized recommendations for improving study routines.
