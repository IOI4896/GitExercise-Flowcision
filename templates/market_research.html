Market Research

1. Forest

Features
  • Focus timer based on Pomodoro technique
  • Gamified tree-growing system to encourage productivity
  • Tracks focus sessions over time
Strengths
  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Strong motivation through gamification
  • Encourage users to avoid distractions
Weaknesses
  • Focuses only on time management
  • Does not consider other factors such as stress or sleep
  • No personalized recommendations

2. Notion

Features
  • Task and project management
  • Customizable dashboards
  • Note-taking and planning tools
Strengths
  • Highly flexible and customizable
  • Suitable for organizing complex workflows
  • Supports long-term planning
Weaknesses
  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • No built-in decision-making or evaluation system
  • Does not provide performance analysis

3. Habitica

Features
  • Habit tracking with RPG-style gamification
  • Daily tasks and rewards system
  • Progress tracking over time
Strengths
  • Engaging and interactive design
  • Encourages habit formation
  • Visual progress system
Weaknesses
  • Focuses on habit tracking rather than evaluation
  • Does not analyze multiple lifestyle factors
  • Limited personalization in recommendations

Conclusion

From the analysis above, most existing applications focus on specific aspects such as time management, task organization, or habit tracking. However, they do not integrate multiple factors such as study time, sleep, focus, and stress into a unified evaluation system.

Our project aims to address this gap by developing a decision-based simulation system that evaluates multiple factors and provides personalized recommendations for improving study routines.
